  • ENGR 111 - Foundations of Engineering I

    Credits 2. 1 Lecture Hour. 3 Lab Hours.

    Introduction to the engineering profession, ethics, and disciplines; development of skills in teamwork, problem solving and design; other topics depending on the major include emphasis on computer applications and programming, visualization and CAD tools, introduction to electrical circuits, semiconductor devices, digital logic, communications and their application in systems; Newton's laws, unit conversions, statistics, computers, Excel; basic graphics skills; visualization and orthographic drawings.

    Prerequisites: MATH 150 or MATH 151, or concurrent enrollment; admission to the College of Engineering.
